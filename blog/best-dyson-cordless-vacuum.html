FAQ

Is higher suction always better?

Not always. Higher suction helps with deep carpet cleaning but drains battery faster and can make the vacuum harder to handle. For hard floors and light cleaning, moderate suction is often enough.

How important is HEPA filtration?

It matters if you are sensitive to dust or allergens. The Gen5detect's 0.1-micron HEPA filtration is the best in the lineup. For general cleaning, the standard advanced filtration in the V15 is usually sufficient.

Can one vacuum replace both sweeping and mopping?

Not completely. The V15s Detect Submarine adds wet cleaning, but still requires separate steps for vacuuming and mopping.

Conclusion

If you want a balanced option that handles most situations well, the Dyson V15 Detect is the most reliable choice. If power is your priority, the Gen5detect delivers the highest suction. If comfort matters more, the V12 Detect Slim offers lightweight handling for smaller spaces. The V8 is a solid budget option, and the V15s Submarine adds wet cleaning for those who want an all-in-one approach. Focus on your floors, your cleaning frequency, and how much weight you can comfortably handle daily.
