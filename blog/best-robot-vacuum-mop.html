Maintenance Tips

  • Clean brushes and rollers weekly — homes with pets may need twice a week
  • Empty and rinse water tanks after each cleaning cycle
  • Wipe sensors and cameras with a soft cloth once a week
  • Replace filters every 1–3 months, mop pads based on wear
  • Keep the dock area free from clutter so the robot can return properly

FAQ

Are robot vacuum mop combos worth it in 2026?

Yes, especially for daily maintenance. Modern models handle both vacuuming and mopping with minimal effort. They don't fully replace deep cleaning, but they reduce how often you need to do it manually.

Can robot vacuums damage carpets with mopping?

They can if the mop stays attached. Advanced models solve this by lifting the mop or dropping it at the dock before touching carpet. This prevents moisture from reaching carpet fibers.

How often should I run a robot vacuum mop?

For best results, vacuum daily or every other day. Mop 2–4 times per week. High-traffic homes may need more frequent cleaning cycles.
