About Me

Hi, I'm Liam Adrian Foster.

I run this website zornavik.me, where I share honest reviews and simple guides about vacuum cleaners. My goal is very clear: I want to help you choose the right vacuum without confusion or waste of money.

My Experience

I have worked in the vacuum cleaner industry for over 12 years. During this time, I worked with different vacuum companies in product testing, quality checking, and customer feedback analysis.

I have seen how vacuums are built, how they perform in real homes, and what problems users often face. This hands-on experience helps me understand which products are truly good and which ones are just marketing.

Why I Started This Website

I started this blog because I saw one big problem.

Most people feel lost when buying a vacuum cleaner. There are too many brands, too many features, and too many fake claims.

I want to make things simple. Here, I break down everything in easy language so you can understand which vacuum is best for your home, which one gives real value for money, and which features actually matter.

My Review Style

I do not just copy product details from brands. I focus on real-world performance.

When I review a vacuum, I look at:

  • Cleaning power
  • Durability
  • Ease of use
  • Noise level
  • Maintenance
  • Value for money

I try to keep everything honest and simple so you can trust my opinion.

Affiliate Transparency

Some of the links on this website are affiliate links. This means if you buy a product through my link, I may earn a small commission.

But this does not change my opinion. I only recommend products that I believe are useful and worth buying.

My Goal

My goal is simple. I want to help you find the right vacuum cleaner for your home without stress or confusion. If my content helps you make a better decision, then my job is done.

Thanks for visiting zornavik.me.
