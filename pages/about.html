Hi, I'm Liam Adrian Foster.

I run zornavik.me, where I share honest reviews and simple guides about vacuum cleaners. My goal is very clear: I want to help you choose the right vacuum without confusion or waste of money.

My Experience

I have worked in the vacuum cleaner industry for over 12 years. During this time, I worked with different vacuum companies in product testing, quality checking, and customer feedback analysis.

I have seen how vacuums are built, how they perform in real homes, and what problems users often face. This hands-on experience helps me understand which products are truly good — and which ones are just marketing.

Why I Started This Website

I started this blog because I saw one big problem: most people feel lost when buying a vacuum cleaner. There are too many brands, too many features, and too many fake claims. I want to make things simple.

Here, I break down everything in plain language so you can understand:

  • Which vacuum is best for your home
  • Which one gives real value for money
  • Which features actually matter
  • Which products you should avoid

My Review Style

I do not just copy product details from brands. I focus on real-world performance. When I review a vacuum, I look at:

  • Cleaning power — not just wattage on a box
  • Durability over months and years of real use
  • Ease of use for everyday cleaning routines
  • Noise level in a real home environment
  • Maintenance requirements and hidden costs
  • Value for money versus the competition

I try to keep everything honest and simple so you can trust my opinion — even if that means telling you not to buy the most expensive model.

⚠ Affiliate Transparency

Some of the links on this website are affiliate links. This means if you buy a product through my link, I may earn a small commission — at no extra cost to you. But this does not change my opinion. I only recommend products that I genuinely believe are useful and worth buying. My reviews are never paid for or influenced by brands.

My Goal

My goal is simple: help you find the right vacuum cleaner for your home without stress or confusion. If my content helps you make a better decision, then my job is done.
