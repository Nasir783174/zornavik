Final Verdict

The right Roomba depends on how much pet hair you deal with and how much effort you want to put into cleaning. If you want maximum automation, the Combo 10 Max stands out. If reliability matters most, the j9+ protects your home from common pet-related issues. For value-focused buyers, the j7+ and i3+ EVO remain practical choices. For multi-floor homes, the i7+ offers the most stable mapping system available.
