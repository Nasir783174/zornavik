Last updated: April 13, 2026

Welcome to zornavik.me. Your privacy is important to me. This page explains what information I collect, how I use it, and your choices when you visit this website.

8. Your Rights

You can ask what data I have about you, request deletion of your personal data, or contact me for any privacy concerns.
