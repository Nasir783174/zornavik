Cordless vacuums have come a long way. Battery life used to be a deal-breaker, but modern models now run 40–60 minutes on a single charge. They're lighter, easier to maneuver, and perfect for quick daily clean-ups.

## How to Choose the Right Cordless Vacuum

### 1. Know Your Floor Type
- **Hardwood/tile:** Any model works; laser detection is a bonus
- **Thick carpet:** You need at least 20,000 Pa suction
- **Mixed floors:** Look for auto-detect modes

### 2. Check Battery Life
- Studio/1-bedroom: 30–40 min is enough
- Larger homes: Look for 50–60 min or swappable batteries

### 3. Consider Weight
- Heavier models (3+ kg) tire you out on long sessions
- Lighter models sacrifice suction power slightly
